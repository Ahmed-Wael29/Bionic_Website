What Is Bionic Team ?

We are Student Organization organizing events for all technologists at Mansura University, like Meetups, Workshops, Events. Our goal is to create a strong Geeks community in Egypt in general, and a calculator and information college in particular. Through partnership with the largest local and international companies, and the largest software engineering workers in Egypt, you will have more information and experience about the labor market and its requirements, and you will be able to strengthen your own Soft Skills, which you will need for the labor market.
